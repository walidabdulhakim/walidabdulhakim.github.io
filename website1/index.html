FAQ

Answers before you hit download.

How do I receive my games?

Once payment is confirmed you’ll receive an email with secure download links, license keys, and a receipt. Links never expire.

What formats are supported?

All games include installers for Windows and macOS, plus Linux builds where applicable. Soundtracks ship as lossless FLAC + MP3.

Can I transfer my license?

Personal licenses are tied to your account but we offer team and commercial plans—email us for custom licensing.

Do you offer refunds?

We provide 14-day refunds if you haven’t activated license keys or downloaded more than 20% of the package.
