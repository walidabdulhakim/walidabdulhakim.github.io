About

Curiosity-driven engineer with a global perspective.

From Afghanistan and educated in UAE and Turkey, I bridge cultures, languages, and disciplines to create technology that empowers people wherever they are.

I fell in love with technology because it gives us tools to solve complex, real-world challenges. During my studies at Istanbul Kultur University, I dove deep into machine learning, signal processing, and full-stack development—disciplines that sharpened my technical rigor and empathy for users.

My projects span biosignal interpretation, blockchain storage, interactive games, and cloud-connected web applications. Each experience helped me develop a pattern: listen, experiment quickly, and build solutions that remain reliable as they scale.

Outside of code, I thrive in collaborative settings where ideas are challenged respectfully. I enjoy exploring emerging research, mentoring peers, and designing workflows that help teams move with clarity.
